We Accept Credits From Everywhere

US or international, accredited or professional — if you earned the credits, we'll evaluate them. Our goal is to transfer as much as possible.

🌎 International Credits

We apply recognized international grade equivalency standards. Transcripts from universities worldwide are evaluated — no need to have studied in the US.

💼 Professional Experience

5+ years of managerial-level work experience can qualify for up to 6 US graduate credits through Prior Learning Assessment.

🎓 Regional & National Schools

Credits from regionally accredited US institutions, national universities, and recognized international schools all qualify for evaluation.

📋 Grade Requirements

Transfer credits need a B or better (or equivalent). Courses must be relevant to your program. We evaluate every transcript individually.
