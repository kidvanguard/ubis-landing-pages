How the Dissertation Track Works

Each phase of your dissertation is a structured $800 track with dedicated faculty mentorship. No ambiguity. No isolation. Just a clear path to done.

TRACK 1 — $800

Dissertation Proposal

Define your research question, scope, and methodology with faculty guidance. Submit a committee-ready proposal with a clear roadmap for your research.

TRACK 2 — $800

Literature Review

Structured literature synthesis with rotating faculty input. Build the theoretical foundation your committee expects, with weekly milestones and feedback.

TRACK 3 — $800

Research & Analysis

Research design, data collection, and analysis with methodologist support. Qualitative, quantitative, or mixed methods — matched with the right mentor.

TRACK 4 — $800

Write-Up & Defense

Complete your manuscript, prepare for defense, and coordinate with your committee. We walk you through every step until you cross the stage.
